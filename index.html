Letter to New Students

Welcome to Virginia Tech! The next four years of your life are going to be filled with adventures, challenges, and life-changing lessons both inside and outside of the classroom. You are going to make lifelong friendships while experiencing a well-rounded and global educational experience against the backdrop of the beautiful blue ridge mountains of southwestern Virginia. Virginia Tech is certainly a unique institution of higher learning, but what separates it from other major land-grant universities? What makes Virginia Tech, well, Virginia Tech? More importantly, what makes Virginia Tech students special?
